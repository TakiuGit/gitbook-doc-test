# Live tv habits

Live TV recommendations are a personalized EPG with an immediate access to the user’s favorites programs.The feature analyzes each user’s TV habits and is connected to the TV programme in order to display a personalized line of on air recommendations.Live TV recommendations identify the next occurrences of the TV shows watched on a regular basis by the user.

### [​](https://spideo-dev-manual.mintlify.app/mgx-recommendation/features/live-tv-habits#endpoint)Endpoint <a href="#endpoint" id="endpoint"></a>

```
GET /users/{user}/linearTvHabits
```

More details on the [API documentation](https://spideo-dev-manual.mintlify.app/dev/null)&#x20;

### **Did you know?**

You can mix this feature with a fallback business rule, in order to show most popular content in case the user doesn’t have enough favorite shows.
